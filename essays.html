My Approach to Essays

I think a good college essay needs to combine eloquence in use of language and clarity in conveying information. Most of essay writing starts with introspection about what you want to say and reflection about how this essay fits into the bigger picture of your application. I think the essay is an incredible opportunity to convey your unique perspective and personality.

Good writing is good editing. I believe in the process of drafting and revising and letting your essay evolve over time. But there's a balance: you don't want to over-edit to the point where your authentic voice no longer shines through in your writing.
