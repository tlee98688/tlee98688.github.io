About

I am an aspiring physician who is interested in science research and communication. Currently, I am starting my senior year at Harvard College, where I double major in Chemistry and English. In my spare time, I like to write and design graphics for The Crimson, blog for the Office for the Arts, and bake.

My latest passion project is working on reimagining my favorite childhood stories with a scientific flair through my blog, The Periodic Fables, which aims to promote science literacy at all age levels.
